About Me

My name is Vo Hoang Anh, and I am from Vietnam, SEA . I have had the privilege of residing in Canada for the past five years, with the last one and a half years spent in the charming city of Ottawa. While Canada has become my 2nd home, I am still acclimating to the colder climate.

Throughout my journey, I have had the opportunity to call several cities across the globe my temporary homes, including Sydney, Queenstown, Auckland, Niagara Falls, Montreal, and, of course, Ottawa. Despite its small size, Ottawa captivates me with its innate beauty, diverse offerings, and has firmly secured its place as one of my most cherished places to live.
